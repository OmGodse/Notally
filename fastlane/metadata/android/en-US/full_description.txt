Notally is a minimalistic note taking app with a beautiful material design and powerful features.

<b>Organization</b>

Create lists to stay on track
Pin notes to always keep them at the top
Color and label your notes for quick organisation
Archive notes to keep them around, but out of your way
Create rich text notes with support for bold, italics, mono space and strike-through
Add clickable links to notes with support for phone numbers, email addresses and web urls

<b>Export notes in the following formats</b>

• PDF
• TXT
• XML
• JSON
• HTML

<b>Convenience</b>

• Auto save
• Dark mode
• Completely free
• No permissions required
• APK size of 1.2 MB (1.6 MB uncompressed)

<b>Privacy</b>

There are no ads, trackers or analytics of any kind. All your notes are stored completely on and never leave your device.

<b>Note</b>

Due to a bug on Xiaomi's part, some MiUI devices might not be able to access the text formatting options.
